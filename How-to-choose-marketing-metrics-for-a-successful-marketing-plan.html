How to choose marketing metrics for a successful marketing plan?

Marketing Metrics are measurable values used by marketing teams to demonstrate the effectiveness of campaigns across all marketing channels. Whether you are looking to track digital marketing performance, SEO progress, or your social media growth, having measurable marketing metrics and KPIs set up can help your business reach targets month-over-month.

Now You got your product ready and now want to reach your target audience. To do that a solid marketing plan is must.

Build Social Plan

How to build solid plan? You need analysis, strategy, marketing mix and metrix setup perfectly to define your objectives underlined in your strategy. Out of all 4 requirements for successful marketing plan, the most important one is your strategy which define your obejectives and last one metrics which define how you measure success of your objective

Key metrics to track:

  1. Financial metrics
    1. Sales and Profit
    2. SQL
    3. Revenue
    4. Net profit
    5. Total opportunity Won
    6. Market share
    7. Billed revenue
    8. Received revenue
    9. Total checkout

  2. Non- financial metrics
    1. Internet Marketing Metrics
    2. Email marketing metrics
    3. Offline Advertisement

  3. Strategic metrics
    1. Brand awareness
    2. Brand perception
    3. Buying intention from brand
    4. Target market positioning

  4. Customer metrics
    1. Customer attraction or new customers added
    2. Customer retention or churn
    3. Customer product usage
    4. Net promoter score

You may not include all metrics in your marketing plan but need to include them as per your objective.

Most important factor which is not spoken yet is marketing budget which will impact our entire marketing plan. We need to figure out all important objectives in qualitative term and map metrics as quantitative term and to get this metrics ready, get estimated budget which will be required in our marketing plan.
